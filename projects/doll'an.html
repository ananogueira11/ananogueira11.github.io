Personal Work

The Doll'AN brand was born in times of pandemic when I started to have more interest in sewing.

It was from there that I started doing some experiments and creating molds for different pieces until I arrived at one of the initial references!

And that's when this project began.

The entire brand process was done by me, from branding to delivery to the client.

Doll'AN Instagram Page



The brand's main products are scrunchies.

They are made by hand and with a sewing machine.

They end up being a practical accessory as well as a beautiful one.
